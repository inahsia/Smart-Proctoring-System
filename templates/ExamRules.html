Rules & Regulations

Monitors your camera and microphone during the exam. Grant permissions when prompted.

If the camera doesn't detect your face, it will be considered a violation.

Ensure the camera can focus on your face at all times during the exam.

Once your face is detected, the system will take a reference photo.

Only you should be visible. Make sure the background is clean and no one else is present.

Exam must be taken in silence. Detected noise will be recorded as a violation.

How to Setup Your Environment

  • Sit at a clean desk or table.
  • Remain in the same room throughout the exam.
  • Maintain a quiet environment—no music or talking.
  • No one else should enter the room.
  • Keep your desk clear of any prohibited items (books, notes, phones, etc.).
